  Infinite Door


When

Fall of Sophomore year

Where

The workshop of my home (the Kappa Sigma - Gamma Pi house)

Why

It's a tradition in our house to paint one's door to reflect their personality. But I wanted to go beyond paint, I wanted to take the design in our house to the next level, and so decided to do this


Details

Prior to the start of the fall semester, my roommates and I built this door from scratch. I made the design, and tried to make it such that any mistakes we made during construction would be hidden. We used a blank door from Home Depot, cut out the center, surrounded the inside with LEDs, cut and hand brushed the aluminium panels and acrylic sheet, and pieced everything together. Another roommate automated the lock with an arduino, such that we could lock and unlock the door from our phones.

Where

Why

Details

In the course 6.090 (Building Programming Experience) my team and I decided to build a game as our final project. This taught me a lot about team work, splitting task, adhering to deadlines, and keeping teammates accountable as well as helping them finish on time.

(Give the game a try! Run 'Final Project.py' to start the game. Requires PyGame)
